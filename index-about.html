About Us

Our Story

Helado's journey began with a simple love for ice cream and a passion for creating something truly different. Tired of the ordinary, we set out to craft artisan ice cream that not only tastes exquisite but tells a story with every spoonful. From only using fresh, organic ingredients of the highest quality, to bringing bold and unique flavour combinations to life— we believe that ice cream should be an experience, not just a dessert. Every batch of our ice cream is made with care, creativity, and a commitment to quality. We share more than just ice cream – we share joy, one scoop at a time.
